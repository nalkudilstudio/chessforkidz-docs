Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: April 2026  ·  🏢 Nalkudil Studio  ·  📧 nalkudilstudio@gmail.com

Our commitment: Chess Master for Kidz is built for children — so privacy is our highest priority. The app works fully offline. We collect no personal data. We show no ads. Your child's learning journey stays entirely on their device.

Section 01

Information We Collect

Chess Master for Kidz stores the following information locally on your device only. This data never leaves the device and is never accessible to Nalkudil Studio.

DataPurpose
Player namePersonalising DR's messages and greetings
Skill level selectionSetting DR's starting difficulty
Game results and ratingShowing progress on the Profile screen
Puzzles solved and streaksTracking learning progress and unlocking tiers
Parental control settingsScreen time and access control (PIN stored as SHA-256 hash)
App preferencesRemembering sound and display settings
Section 02

What We Never Do

✓ No ads✓ No in-app purchases✓ No analytics✓ No account required✓ No data uploaded✓ No data sold✓ No social features✓ No AI training on content

We do not collect names, email addresses, location data, device identifiers, advertising IDs, crash reports, photos, camera or microphone access, or any data from children under 13.

Section 03

Internet Access

Chess Master for Kidz is 100% offline. All puzzles, lessons, and games work without any internet connection. The app makes zero network requests and does not communicate with any external server.

Section 04

Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

This app is designed for children aged 6–14. We take their privacy more seriously than anything else.

  • We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children
  • We do not require account creation or login of any kind
  • We do not include social features, chat, or external user-generated content
  • We do not display advertisements of any kind
  • All data can be permanently deleted by uninstalling the app
Section 05

Parental Controls

Built-in parental controls are accessible from Settings → Parental Controls.

FeatureDescription
🔒 PIN Protection4-digit PIN to restrict settings access. Stored as a one-way SHA-256 hash — never recoverable.
⏱️ Screen Time LimitsSet daily play time limits. DR reminds your child when time is up.
📊 Progress DashboardWeekly activity, rating trends, puzzles solved — visible only to parents.
👆 Fingerprint UnlockBiometric alternative to PIN, processed entirely on-device.
