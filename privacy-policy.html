Privacy Policy

📅 Last updated: March 2026 🏢 Nalkudil Studio 📧 nalkudilstudio@gmail.com
Our commitment: Chess Master for Kidz is built for children — so privacy is our highest priority. The app works fully offline. We collect no personal data. We show no ads. Your child's learning journey stays entirely on their device.

Section 01

Information We Collect

Chess Master for Kidz stores the following information locally on your device only. This data never leaves the device, is never uploaded to any server, and is not accessible to Nalkudil Studio.

DataPurpose
Player namePersonalising DR's messages and greetings
Skill level selectionSetting the starting DR difficulty
Game results and ratingShowing progress on the Profile screen
Puzzles solved and streaksTracking learning progress
Parental control settingsScreen time and access control
App preferencesRemembering voice and timer settings
Section 02

What We Never Do

✓ No ads ✓ No in-app purchases ✓ No analytics ✓ No account required ✓ No data sold ✓ No data shared ✓ No social features ✓ No AI training on content

We do not collect names, email addresses, location data, device identifiers, advertising IDs, usage analytics, crash reports, photos, camera or microphone access, or any data from children under 13.

Section 03

Internet Access

Chess Master for Kidz is 100% offline. All chess puzzles, opening lessons, and game features work without any internet connection. The app makes zero network requests during normal use and does not communicate with external servers, analytics services, or advertising networks.
Section 04

Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

This app is designed for children aged 6–14. We take their privacy more seriously than anything else.

  • We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children
  • We do not require account creation or login of any kind
  • We do not include social features, chat, or user-generated content
  • We do not display advertisements of any kind
  • Parents can review all data stored by going to Settings → Parental Controls
  • All data can be permanently deleted by uninstalling the app
Section 05

Parental Controls

The app includes built-in parental controls accessible from Settings → Parental Controls.

🔒

PIN Protection

Set a 4-digit PIN to restrict access to settings. Stored as a one-way SHA-256 hash — we cannot recover it.

⏱️

Screen Time Limits

Set daily play time limits. DR reminds your child to take a break when time is up.

📊

Progress Dashboard

Weekly activity, rating trends, puzzles solved — visible only to you.

👆

Fingerprint Unlock

Enable biometric authentication as an alternative to PIN entry.

Section 07

Data Deletion

To delete all data stored by the app, simply uninstall the app from your device. No data is stored remotely, so uninstalling removes everything completely and permanently.

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be published at this page and reflected in the next app update. We will not retroactively reduce protections for data already collected without appropriate notice.
