Getting Started

Up & Running in 3 Steps

From download to your first game — here's exactly how to get started.

01

📲 Download & Enter Your Name

Get Chess Master for Kidz from the Play Store — no account or login needed.

  • Search "Chess Master for Kidz" on Play Store
  • Tap Install → Open
  • Enter your child's player name
  • DR greets you personally from that point on
  • No email, no sign-in, no permissions required
02

🎯 Choose Your Level

DR adapts to your child's ability. Start at Beginner and level up as confidence grows.

  • Tap Settings → Difficulty
  • 5 levels: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert, Grandmaster
  • Beginner plays very randomly — perfect for first-timers
  • Level up anytime from Settings
  • DR always encourages — never frustrates
03

♟️ Start Playing & Solving

Jump into a game against DR, solve puzzles, or explore openings — all fully offline.

  • Tap Play to start a game against DR
  • Visit Puzzles for daily tactical challenges
  • Earn rating points with every win and puzzle solved
  • Unlock new avatars as you improve
  • Set daily time limits in Settings → Parental Controls

Feature Guide

Know Your Tools

🤖
AI Opponent

DR — Your Chess Guide

DR is your warm, encouraging chess tutor. 5 difficulty levels powered by Stockfish — from 80% random moves for beginners to 8-second think time for Grandmaster.

  • Beginner: very random, great for first-timers
  • Expert to Grandmaster: serious challenge
  • DR gives calm, encouraging feedback always

Learning

Puzzle Mode

259 hand-picked Lichess puzzles across 5 unlockable tiers — from simple forks to mate-in-3.

  • Tier 1 unlocked from day one

  • Daily limit of 25 puzzles — resets at midnight

FAQ

Common Questions

Does the app work without internet?
Yes — Chess Master for Kidz is 100% offline. All puzzles, lessons, and games are built into the app. No internet connection is ever required.
What age is the app designed for?
The app is designed for children aged 6–14. The Beginner level is ideal for first-timers, while Grandmaster difficulty can challenge older, more advanced players.
How does my child's rating work?
The rating starts at 500 and adjusts based on game results and puzzle performance. Winning against a tougher DR level earns more points.
How do I set up parental controls?
Open Settings → Parental Controls. Set a 4-digit PIN, configure daily screen time limits, and view your child's weekly progress dashboard.
Is there a paid version or in-app purchases?
No. Chess Master for Kidz is completely free with no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or advertising of any kind.
