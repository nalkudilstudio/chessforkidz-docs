Getting Started
Up & Running in 3 Steps
01

📲 Download & Enter Your Name

Get Chess Master for Kidz from the Google Play Store — no account or login needed.

  • Search "Chess Master for Kidz" on Play Store
  • Tap Install → Open
  • Enter your child's player name
  • DR greets you personally from that point on
  • No email, no sign-in, no permissions needed
02

🎯 Choose Your Level

DR adapts to your child's ability. Start at Beginner and level up as confidence grows.

  • Tap Settings → Difficulty
  • 5 levels: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert, Grandmaster
  • Beginner plays very randomly — perfect for first-timers
  • Level up anytime from Settings
  • DR always encourages — never frustrates
03

♚ Start Playing & Solving

Jump into a game against DR, solve puzzles, or explore chess openings — all offline.

  • Tap Play to start a game against DR
  • Visit Puzzles for daily tactical challenges
  • Earn rating points with every win and puzzle solved
  • Unlock new avatars as you improve
  • Parent? Set daily time limits in Settings → Parental Controls
Feature Guide
Know Your Tools
🤖
AI Opponent

DR — Your Chess Guide

DR is your warm, calm, and encouraging chess tutor. 5 difficulty levels powered by the Stockfish engine — from 80% random moves for beginners to 8-second thinking time for Grandmaster.

  • Beginner: very random, great for learning
  • Expert/Master/Grandmaster: real challenge
  • DR always explains moves in a friendly way
🧩
Learning

Puzzle Mode

259 hand-picked Lichess puzzles across 5 unlockable tiers. Solve tactical puzzles to sharpen your pattern recognition — from simple forks to mate-in-3 sequences.

  • Tier 1 unlocked from day one
  • Unlock Tier 2 at 5 solves, Tier 3 at 15, and so on
  • Daily limit of 25 puzzles — resets at midnight
  • Puzzles rated by difficulty (Lichess rating)
♟️
Progression

Rating & Avatars

Your rating grows with every win and puzzle solved. Unlock new chess piece avatars as milestones are reached — from Pawn all the way to King.

  • Start as a Pawn ♟ — the default avatar
  • Knight ♞ unlocks at 10 puzzles solved
  • Rook ♜ at 10 wins, Queen ♛ at rating 600+
  • King ♚ — the ultimate avatar — at 1000+ rating

FAQ
Common Questions
Does the app work without internet?
Yes — Chess Master for Kidz is 100% offline. All puzzles, lessons, and games are built into the app. No internet connection is ever required.
What age is the app designed for?
The app is designed for children aged 6–14. The Beginner level is suitable for first-timers, while Grandmaster difficulty can challenge older, more advanced players.
How does my child's rating work?
The rating starts at 500 and adjusts based on game results and puzzle performance. Winning against a tougher DR level earns more points. It's based on a simplified Elo-style system.
How do I set up parental controls?
Open Settings → Parental Controls. Set a 4-digit PIN to lock settings, configure daily screen time limits, and view your child's weekly progress dashboard.
Can my child lose their progress?
Progress is stored locally on the device. If the app is uninstalled, data is cleared. We recommend occasional manual progress review via the parental controls dashboard.
Is there a paid version or in-app purchases?
No. Chess Master for Kidz is completely free with no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or advertising of any kind.
