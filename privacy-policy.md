# Privacy Policy — Chess Master for Kidz

**Last updated:** March 2026  
**App:** Chess Master for Kidz  
**Developer:** Nalkudil Studio  
**Contact:** nalkudilstudio@gmail.com

---

## 1. Overview

Chess Master for Kidz ("the App") is an offline chess learning app for children developed by Nalkudil Studio. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This policy explains clearly what data the App uses, where it stays, and what we never do.

**Short version:** The App works fully offline, collects no personal data, shows no ads, and never connects to the internet during normal use.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Data stored only on your device

The App stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Player name (entered during setup) | Personalising DR's messages |
| Skill level selection | Setting the starting difficulty |
| Game results and rating | Showing progress on the Profile screen |
| Puzzles solved and streaks | Tracking learning progress |
| Parental control settings and PIN hash | Screen time and access control |
| App preferences (voice on/off, timer setting) | Remembering your choices |

**This data never leaves your device.** It is not uploaded to any server, not shared with any third party, and not accessible to Nalkudil Studio.

### 2.2 Data we do NOT collect

We do not collect:

- Names, email addresses, or any contact information
- Location data
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Photos, camera, or microphone access
- Any data from children under 13

---

## 3. Internet and Network Access

Chess Master for Kidz **does not require an internet connection** to play. All chess puzzles, opening lessons, and game features work fully offline.

The App does not make any network requests during normal use. It does not communicate with external servers, analytics services, or advertising networks.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

This App is designed for children aged 6–14 and we take their privacy seriously.

- We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children
- We do not require account creation or login
- We do not include social features, chat, or user-generated content
- We do not display advertisements
- We do not include in-app purchases
- Parents can review all data stored by the App by going to **Settings → Parental Controls**
- All data can be permanently deleted by uninstalling the App

---

## 5. Parental Controls

The App includes a built-in Parental Controls section (Settings → Parental Controls) that allows parents to:

- Set a PIN to restrict access to settings
- Set daily screen time limits
- View the child's weekly progress
- Enable fingerprint unlock

The parent PIN is stored as a one-way SHA-256 hash on the device. Nalkudil Studio cannot recover or access this PIN.

To delete all data stored by the App, simply **uninstall the App** from your device. No data is stored remotely, so uninstalling removes everything completely.
